Sushi Cat

This game is a solo project made in Flash with ActionScript3. I made it in Cegep and polished it a little after.

About:

Sushi Cat is a simple game meant for tablets played in short sessions. The player controls a cat in a sushi restaurant and needs to knock off food from the waiters using a lint ball in order to survive.
