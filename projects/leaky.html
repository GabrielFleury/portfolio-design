Leaky's Escape

This is a mobile game concept I'm working on as a solo project. I have a Game Design Document and a prototype, and I am currently iterating on it.

About:

Leaky's Escape is a mobile game designed for short play sessions and only requires one hand. The player controls an Alien trying to escape Area 51 using his tongue to slingshot himself over platforms. It is a game of quick reflexes where the player needs to keep their momentum while capturing Goo balls to heal and dodging obstacles.
